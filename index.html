ℹ️ About Gait Analysis

Gait analysis is the systematic study of human walking. By tracking parameters like stride length, walking speed, cadence, and joint angles, clinicians identify imbalances, monitor rehabilitation progress, and optimize athletic performance.

Key measurement techniques:

  • 📐 Kinematics — Markers, cameras, GPS, accelerometers
  • ⚖️ Kinetics — Force platforms, pressure insoles, footswitches
  • ⚡ EMG — Muscle activation patterns

Gait Cycle Phases:
■ Stance (~60%) — Foot on ground
■ Swing (~40%) — Foot in air
